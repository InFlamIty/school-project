How we Made a Coin Battery





We followed this video guide on how to make coin battery. So if you don't want to read, you can watch the video. link | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vIHfUJu3aKo


So, if your still here, here are the following steps we took to make the coin battery


1. we got 10 pennies and soaked them in a mix of distilled vinegar and some salt for about a minute
2. we dried the coins and used 1 to create 10 identical circles in thin cardboard and cut the circles out
3. once we finished cutting out all the cardboard circles, we repeated step 2 but on tin foil
4. we repeated step 1 but with the cardboard and we left it there until we started building
Now that we are done all of that we started to actually build the battery.
5. first we got a penny and placed it down on a flat surface
6. after that we put down a cardboard circle on the penny
7. then we placed a tin foil cirlce on the cardboard circle
8. we repeated steps 5, 6, and 7 until we ran out of pennies
9. once we finished that we connect a wire to the top and the bottom
10. after we placed the wires we taped the entire thing together

And finally we finished the battery, all we have to do now is connect the wires to something like a light or a buzzer and it will work!
